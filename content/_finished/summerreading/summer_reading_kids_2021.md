<h1 style="font-size:2.1em;">Hello, fellow travelers!</h1>
This summer you will complete reading and activity challenges as you make your way across the Summer Reading maps from Darien, Connecticut all the way to San Francisco, California! 

There are two different paths to crossing the country: a game for children from birth to kindergarten and another for children in kindergarten through sixth grade. 

The fuel for your roadtrip is to complete reading challenges. You can read any way you want from checking out physical books, downloading e-Books, or listening to audiobooks. Everyone who registers will receive a free book that they can pick up at the Children's Library.
